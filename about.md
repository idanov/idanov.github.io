I am a software engineer based in London. I received my _MSc_ in _Computer Science and Engineering_ from **[DTU](https://www.dtu.dk/english)** in 2013. My first programming endeavours date as far back as year 2000 when I used my friend's **[Apple II replica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pravetz_computers)** to try out some coding examples from a couple of very old books on _[BASIC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BASIC)_. My professional career as a programmer started a few months before finishing [high school](http://www.elsys-bg.org/) and continued throughout my [undergraduate studies](http://www.tu-sofia.bg/).

I fill my long commute to work with listening to audiobook classics or reading software engineering books. I try to dedicate some of my spare time to learning more about _Machine Learning_, _Artificial Intelligence_, _Algorithms_ and _Functional Programming_. Currently my technologies of choice include _Scala_, _Akka_, _Docker_, _ReactJS_, _Jupyter_ and _SciPy_. I would love to have more free time to experiment with _Deep Learning_ using _Theano_ or _TensorFlow_. I want to make a big and positive impact on the world, but so does everyone else I guess :smile:
